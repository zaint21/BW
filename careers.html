Careers at Boardwalk Insurance

About Boardwalk Insurance

Boardwalk is going to change the way people think about Commercial Insurance in Canada.

At Boardwalk, we are changing the way people purchase, service, and process data across the insurance industry. The insurance industry is trapped in legacy systems and still uses pen and paper for all of their applications. We want to eliminate the old ways and make the customer’s insurance buying experience as painless as possible. We have an idea of how this looks like but we haven't figured it all out yet. Join us to figure this out together!

Why Join Us?

You'll get a lot of autonomy, responsibility, and ownership. We will give you challenging goals and lots of context and guidance, but we’ll expect you to employ good judgement and figure out the details of how you will get there. We're an early stage startup and you'll be working directly with the founders, helping define vision, product, and user experience. Join us as one of our key early engineering, sales, or customer success hires and be part of our core team.

Who You Are?

You take initiative and prioritize the work that adds the most value to the company. You thrive in a fast-paced environment. You have great focus, you take ownership, and you get things done. You are constantly learning and always improving. You care deeply about the user experience, about our customers, and about our company. You genuinely appreciate well-intentioned critical feedback and you give as much of it as you receive, because that's how we continue to grow together. You communicate with great clarity and high insight density. You bring new ideas and you offer suggestions when you think things can be improved.

Full Stack Software Engineer

As a Software Engineer, Fullstack, you will work with the founders alongside other backend engineers. You will design and oversee the development of new features, enhance the user experience, and optimize the product for scalability.

Other than working on the codebase and infrastructure, you will take part in the product road map and setting up the right processes to help us scale the product. Your ideas, feedback, and critique are highly important to us.

Responsibilities:
  • Fullstack development (.Net Core)
  • Proficiency in React/Redux, Express, Webpack
  • Design and development of REST APIs
  • Planning, scaling, and deploying application on AWS and related cloud infrastructure
  • Third party API integration (Hubspot, Twilio, Typeform, Formspree, Calendly)
  • Design, test, deploy and maintain codebase

Minimum qualifications:
  • You write clean, testable and maintainable code
  • Proficient with .Net Core Framework
  • Experience designing REST APIs
  • Good eye for UI design and UX
  • Experience designing scalable systems
  • Ability to readily learn most technologies on-the-go
  • Ability to thrive in an fast-paced environment with a high degree of autonomy
  • Verbal and written English fluency

Benefits and Perks:
  • Competitive Salary and equity compensation
  • Comprehensive benefits package including physical and mental health, vision, dental, and other paramedical services
  • Team building events every month

Business Development Manager

As a Business Development Manager, you will be responsible for the overall team and individual performance as related to obtaining revenue goals and business objectives. This will include identifying KPIs and key activities as well as addressing any corrective measures that promote team and individual success and growth.

Responsibilities:

  • Monitor real-time outbound and inbound sales performance and address any areas of concern.
  • Participate in product development activities and testing.
  • Assist Business Development Rep to negotiate new business terms with market underwriters.
  • Identify new target classes for new marketing campaigns.
  • Assist in developing various online Marketing Initiatives.
  • Report, track, and manage sales activities and results.
  • Ensure ongoing improvements and feedback are included and presented to the entire team.
  • Develop processes for tracking and documenting sales team activities.
  • Provide input to the development of annual sales targets, performance metrics, recognition programs, compensation programs/bonus and KPIs.
  • Attend various industry events to represent Boardwalk Insurance to obtain market insight for the team.

Minimum qualifications:
  • Minimum 3-5 years of experience in sales
  • Management experience required and previous experience in a call center environment an asset.
  • RIBO Licensed preferred
  • Knowledge and understanding of call center performance metrics in an Outbound and Inbound sales structure.
  • Experience with fundamental financial analysis (cost-effectiveness, cost-benefit, etc.)
  • Solid organizational, leadership, and problem-solving skills.
  • Excellent listening, interpersonal, written and oral communication skills.
  • Strong understanding of P&C insurance products.
  • Post-secondary education or equivalent business experience is ideal.

Benefits and Perks:
  • Competitive Salary and equity compensation
  • Comprehensive benefits package including physical and mental health, vision, dental, and other paramedical services
  • Team building events every month

Business Development Representative

A Business Development Representative at Boardwalk Insurance is about creating and maintaining relationships with our Insurance partners and customers. We are looking for seasoned sales professionals specifically in the Toronto region.

Responsibilities:

  • Develop new relationships from inbound leads and strategic outreach.
  • Identify target prospects for new outbound campaigns.
  • Negotiate terms and conditions with market underwriters.
  • Meet target quota of qualified opportunities, value of sales pipeline, and closed business.
  • Maintain a deep understanding of our products and services (insurance and technology) and speak with business owners about the most relevant coverages/features/functionality for their specific business needs
  • Monitor performance and identify trends in Boardwalk Insurance adoption and provide guidance to Business clients as part of their periodic Success Review.

Minimum qualifications:
  • Minimum of 1-2 years of previous experience selling P&C insurance
  • Self-motivated; entrepreneurial spirit
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills (verbal & written)
  • Aptitude to learn technical concepts/terms
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks/projects simultaneously

Benefits and Perks:
  • Competitive Salary and equity compensation
  • Comprehensive benefits package including physical and mental health, vision, dental, and other paramedical services
  • Team building events every month

Customer Success Representative

A Customer Success representative at Boardwalk Insurance is about maintaining and building with our Insurance partners and customers. Handling customer and underwriter requests professionally and efficiently.

Responsibilities:

  • Resolve customer inquiries and requests
  • Provide quick and accurate service
  • Knowledge and understanding of commercial insurance coverages, practices and policies Collaborate with the sales team with onboarding new customers
  • Communicate with Insurance companies’ underwriters. To complete a policy adjustment request.
  • Assist u/w with inquiries about clients. Clarify and confirm information.
  • Ability to identify and inform clients of potential coverage enhancement opportunities and deficiencies
  • Work directly with other departments and coworkers to build productive internal working relationships to effectively resolve customer-raised issues

Minimum qualifications:
  • Minimum of 1-2 years of previous experience servicing P&C insurance
  • Self-motivated; entrepreneurial spirit
  • Comfortable working in a fast-paced, dynamic environment
  • Strong interpersonal communication skills (verbal & written)
  • Strong organizational and time management skills
