Can I get an instant quote online?

Yes, you can apply for a quote online. It isn’t instant yet, but we are working with our developers and insurance partners to bring you that functionality soon. In the meantime, we will get you a quote back as quickly as possible! We value your time sensitive needs.

Are you an insurance company? Who underwrites my policy?

We are an insurance brokerage. We collect your risk information and negotiate the best insurance policy for your business. We have access to dozens of insurers eager to help.

Can I speak to a person?

Of course! Bots are not much for conversation. Navigating an endless automated telephone directory isn’t much fun either. We love to chat, give us a call, message us online, send us an email. We are even available to meet with you in person if that is your preference, granted Mr. Ford allows it

I have a policy now and I think my broker is doing a good job. Why change?

If it ain’t broke, don’t fix it. Unfortunately, your insurance policy is not a bicycle. Policy deficiencies, improper programming and harmful exclusions usually go unnoticed until it is too late. These problems typically surface at time of a claim, and by that point, you are paying the price for them. Don’t wait until then, let us get you on the right policy for your business.

Are you an insurance brokerage or a tech company?

The famous question our investors loved asking us. Boardwalk is an Insuretech company. However, our roots are deeply ingrained in insurance. Our founders have come from the insurance space, and have an extensive background in it. Technology is our enabler, our way to improve efficiencies, deliver a superior customer experience and differentiate ourselves from our peers. Both are top priority for us, obviously after our customers.

Where do you operate?

We are domiciled just outside Toronto, Ontario but write business across Canada.

We are licensed in the following provinces: Ontario, Alberta, Saskatchewan, British Columbia, Manitoba, Nova Scotia, Prince Edward Island, & Newfoundland & New Brunswick.

I have had a claim, who do I call?

Ghostbusters. Sorry, we had too. Most policies will indicate a toll free number to call in the event of a claim. You can retrieve this from your policy documents and is usually on the first or second page. Alternatively, you may use our online claims form and we will get the ball rolling.
